Introduction to Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift, born in Pennsylvania on December 13, 1989, is an American female singer, songwriter, music producer and actor.

In 2006, she released her first album, Taylor Swift, which was certified by the American record industry association and sold five times as many platinum records. In 2008, released the music album Fearless, which won the 11 week champion on the billboard album list of the United States, certified 7 times the platinum record sales, and won the annual album award of the 52nd Grammy Awards. In 2010, she released the music album Speak Now, and the single "mean" won two Grammy Awards. In the same year, she was awarded the star light award by the creator celebrity don Hal David. In 2011, with the sales of three physical albums, it won the "five platinum records" sales certification in China. In 2012, she began to transform to a pop singer and released the music album Red, which was certified as a "double platinum record" in China. In 2013, she won the 47th Country Music Association Award.

In 2014, the album 1989 was released, which won the 58th Grammy Award for album of the year. The International Federation of Phonographic Industry announced that the album was the "global record sales champion of the year". In the same year, she won the Dick Clark lifetime achievement award of the National Music Award and was selected as the person of the year of time (American Edition). In April 2015, she was awarded the 50th anniversary milestone award of the 50th Country Music Academy Award. In 2017, the music album reputation was released. With 1.238 million copies sold in the first week of the United States, she became a singer with four million sales in the first week. In the same year, she was selected as the person of the year by time magazine. In 2019, the music album Lover will be released; in the same year, the International Federation of Phonographic Industry announced that the album was "the world record sales champion of the year". In 2020, the music album folklore and evermore will be released.The former won the annual album award of the 63rd Grammy Awards.
